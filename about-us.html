About LeaRNfromNurses

LeaRNfromNurses is a health website developed by a group of Level II Bachelor of Science in Nursing students from Chinese General Hospital Colleges. The team was brought together with a goal in mind, and that was to provide basic health information regarding the use of substances during pregnancy. As nursing students, our mission is to teach, preach, and enrich awareness, consciousness, and understanding of the various substances that negatively affect pregnancy.

At LeaRNfromNurses , we believe that it is your tuRN to leaRN knowledge about substance use and disceRN insights about the proper health behaviors and practices during pregnancy.

Get closer to leaRNfromNurses

The Chinese General Hospital Colleges is a medical university located in Manila, Philippines. It was established in 1921 as the Chinese General Hospital School of Nursing (CGHSN). It is owned and managed by the Philippine Chinese Charitable Association (PCCA) Inc., as a non-profit service organization. The College offer courses such as Bachelor of Science in Nursing, Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology, BS in Psychology, BS in Radiologic Technology and Diploma in Midwifery.
